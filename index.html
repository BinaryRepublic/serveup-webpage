Our Vision

Our product will enhance the communication between the guest and the waiter in restaurants to avoid inefficiencies and therefore increases customer satisfaction and sales. You probably have experienced this situation yourself already. Just imagine: You are sitting in a restaurant and you want to order another drink. However all the waiters are as usual really busy and it takes a while to get hold of the attention of one of the waiters. Our solution: A voice assistant that is placed on each table that gives the restaurant guests the opportunity to order drinks whenever they want.
